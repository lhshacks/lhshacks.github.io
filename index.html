About (New website in progress)

VikeHacks is Lynbrook High School's student hackathon. Join us for 12 hours of hacking, learning, and prizes!

VikeHacks is focused completely on one goal: helping you build the project of your dreams. From hardware hacking to website development to game design, you will be able to build it at VikeHacks.

In fact, VikeHacks is perfect even if you're completely new to computer programming! Not only will we be providing beginner workshops in all sorts of programming, but we will have personal mentors available to help you get started on building your dreams.
